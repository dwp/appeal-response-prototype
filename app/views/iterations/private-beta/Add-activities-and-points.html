Add activities and points (new feature)

Hypothesis / Experiment

We have seen that

Appeal Writers manually add this to the response, which requires a lot of editing and removing conditions

So if we

Make it easier for Appeal Writers to enter activities and points in our service

We will see

Less manual editing of the response

What's new or changed?

Existing design

Appeal writers were manually editing the activities and points awarded in the word document.




New design

We got early feedback on design concepts for this section of the Appeal response






We found that quite often no points were awarded which led us to add a question upfront to prevent Appeal Writers entering all '0s'

We also found that 'eating or drinking' didn't belong in either physical or mental activites and instead should be a seperate question






What did we learn?

  • In prototype testing participants were able to add and remove conditions with ease
  • There were no usability issues identified although we did only test the 'happy path'
  • We have enough confidence in the design to test it in Live, due to go in March 24
