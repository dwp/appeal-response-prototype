Changes to the health and disability fact

Hypothesis / Experiment

We have seen that

Through feedback we have found Appeal Writers re-entering the same health consitions previously declared.

We think this is because

‘Change to health or disability’ is one of the most commonly used Facts of the case.

So if we

Make it easier for Appeal Writers to enter multiple changes to health or disability

We will see

More positive feedback and a reduction in time spent in the service

What's new or changed?

Existing design





New design

We started with the DWP 'add another thing pattern'




Allowing users to add any new health conditions or remove any exisiting without having to re-enter them each time






What did we learn?

  • In prototype testing participants were able to add and remove conditions with ease
  • There were no usability issues identified although we did only test the 'happy path'
  • We have enough confidence in the design to test it in Live, due to go in March 24
